Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a ancient recipe created by somebody's grandmother and has lived till this day to make delicious meatballs.

Ingredients

  • 400g of minced meat
  • 2 tsp of salt
  • 3 tsp of seasonings
  • lasagna plates

Steps

  1. Mix the Ingredients
  2. Shape them into a mold
  3. Cook the lasagna in the oven for 30mins
