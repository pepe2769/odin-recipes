Meatballs recipe

Description

This is a ancient recipe created by somebody's grandmother and has lived till this day to make delicious meatballs.

Ingredients

  • 400g of minced meat
  • 2 tsp of salt
  • 3 tsp of seasonings
  • 2 cups of breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Mix the Ingredients
  2. Shape them into balls
  3. Cook them in the oven for 20mins
